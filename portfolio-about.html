I’m interested in how we derive meaning from the mathematical and cosmological dimensions of rhythmic and harmonic organization – habits of being, sacred and secular ritual, songwriting as ceremony and the visceral confines of contemporary pop music. The majority of my work sets digital technology as the primary medium used to investigate the unseen and the role sound plays in shaping our reality. I live in Los Angeles, CA where I’m working on a global peace initiative with David Lynch. As a musician, I’ve worked and collaborated with a variety of labels and media outlets ranging from Paw Tracks Records to Vogue Paris. I’ve toured extensively throughout North America and Europe, performing at numerous festivals (e.g. All Tomorrow’s Parties, Sasquatch!, Midi Festival) and venues (e.g. J. Paul Getty Museum, Bowery Ballroom NYC). My sound work has been hosted by spaces such as the Knoxville Museum of Art, Queens Museum Biennial, and The Hunter Museum.
